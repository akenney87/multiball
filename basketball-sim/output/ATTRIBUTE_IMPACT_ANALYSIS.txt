RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================================================

UNDERUTILIZED ATTRIBUTES:
    - Durability: Should be integrated into injury system (Milestone 3)
    - Patience: Could affect:
        * Shot selection (patient players take better shots)
        * Drive timing (waiting for openings)
        * Offensive decision-making speed

    - Top Speed: Only used in transition (20%)
        * Could affect: Fast break defense, getting back on D

    - Creativity: Only used in block avoidance (10%)
        * Could affect: Shot variety, unconventional finishes

    - Deception: Only used in block avoidance (7%)
        * Could affect: Drive success, getting open

    - Core Strength: Only used in rebounding (14%)
        * Could affect: Contact finishes, post defense


WELL-INTEGRATED ATTRIBUTES:
    - Hand-Eye Coordination: 8 actions, clearly impacts ball skills
    - Agility: 8 actions, affects movement in all contexts
    - Jumping: 7 actions, dominant in rim play
    - Composure: 7 actions, critical under pressure
    - Height: 6 actions, defensive and rebounding impact
    - Awareness: 6 actions, decision-making across systems
